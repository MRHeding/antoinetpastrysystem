About Antoinette's

Founded in April 2017 by Mr. Adolfo T. Basilio, Antoinette's Pastries has been serving the Zamboanga City community for nearly nine years from our location on Mercedes Socorro Street. Under Mr. Adolfo's experienced management and Ms. Antoinette's skilled baking, we've built a reputation for quality pastries that have earned us a loyal customer base throughout the years.
